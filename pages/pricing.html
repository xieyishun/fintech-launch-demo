Frequently asked

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is CreatorBank a real bank?

CreatorBank is a fintech platform that partners with FDIC-insured banking institutions to provide deposit accounts, payment processing, and lending services. Your money is held with our partner banks and is FDIC-insured up to $250,000 per account.

How do you connect to YouTube, Twitch, and other platforms?

We use official OAuth APIs from each platform — meaning you grant us read-only access to your earnings data. We never see your platform passwords, and you can revoke access anytime from your settings. We never post on your behalf.

Can I really get a loan based on my creator earnings?

Yes! Our underwriting model is built specifically for creator income. We look at platform earnings history, audience growth, content consistency, and platform diversification — not traditional W-2 employment. Most Pro users qualify for loans up to $50K with no hard credit check required.

What if I'm not in the US?

Right now, CreatorBank is US-only while we navigate the regulatory landscape. We're actively working on launching in Canada, the UK, and the EU in late 2026. Join the waitlist from our homepage to be notified.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel from settings in two clicks. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period, and we'll export your data in CSV format on request. No questions asked, no retention pressure.

Is my financial data secure?

We use 256-bit AES encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, PCI DSS Level 1 compliant for payment data, and undergo quarterly penetration testing by third-party security firms. Your data is yours — we never sell it to advertisers.
