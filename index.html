01 · About Lumen

A new kind of advisor for a new kind of investor.

Most Americans under 40 have never spoken to a financial advisor. Not because they don't want guidance — but because the industry was built for someone else: older, wealthier, comfortable with paperwork and 1% fees.

Lumen AI rebuilds the advisor from the ground up. We pair an institutional-grade portfolio engine with a language-model interface that explains every decision in plain English. No minimums. No commissions. No jargon.

We're headquartered in Toronto and New York, regulated in both the U.S. and Canada, and on a mission to make intelligent investing the default — not the privilege.

02 · The Product

Four engines. One portfolio. Yours.

Lumen AI isn't a single algorithm — it's a coordinated system of models that handle the unglamorous work great advisors used to do by hand.

Adaptive Portfolio Engine

Reinforcement-learning models continuously rebalance your holdings against real-time market regimes — not a static target you set on day one.

Tax-Loss Harvesting+

Every day, our system scans for harvest opportunities across taxable accounts and replaces sold lots with correlated alternatives — adding an estimated 0.8–1.4% in after-tax returns annually.

Conversational Co-Pilot

Ask "should I max out my Roth before paying down my student loans?" — and get a personalized, regulation-compliant answer in seconds, grounded in your actual financial data.

Goal-Based Planning

Buying a house in Austin in 2028? Sending a kid to UBC in 2035? Lumen builds parallel sub-portfolios for each goal, each with its own risk curve and glide path.

Bank-Grade Security

Assets custodied at Apex Clearing (US) and CI Investment Services (CA). 256-bit encryption, biometric login, and SIPC / CIPF insurance up to $500K per account.

Explainable AI

Every trade comes with a plain-English rationale. No black boxes. Audit trail and regulator-ready reports available on demand.
